In Edit is a simple way to hide a spoiler on Facebook. It is often used in Game Show groups to hide solutions to problems.

iOS

Using In Edit

Long-Press on the comment to open the action menu, then press Edit.

Then edit the comment to say "In Edit", or something similar.

Revealing In Edit

Long-Press on the comment to open the action menu, then press View Edit History.
